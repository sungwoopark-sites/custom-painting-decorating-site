About Us

Built on Family Values

Custom Painting & Decorating Inc. grew from a family legacy rooted in hard work and genuine skill. Cesar Munoz learned the trade while helping his father on painting jobs across Southern California, gaining hands-on experience that shaped his understanding of quality and client care.

His commitment to building something meaningful for his family pushed him to earn his painting license, opening the path to a business defined by integrity and craftsmanship. In 2018, Cesar and his father turned their shared passion into a family-operated company dedicated to serving homeowners and businesses throughout Orange County.

Their team brings exceptional attention to detail, reliable communication, and a deep sense of pride to every project. Today, the company continues to honor its roots by delivering personalized service and lasting results that elevate every space.

Everything You Need to Know

What painting services do you offer in Orange County?

We provide complete house painting services for residential and commercial properties across Orange County, CA. Our services include interior painting, exterior house painting, cabinet painting, drywall installation and repair, and specialty finishes. Each painting project is tailored to your specific needs, property type, and timeline.

Are you licensed and insured painting contractors?

Yes, Custom Painting & Decorating Inc. is fully licensed and insured in California. Our local painting company follows industry standards for safety, preparation, and application. This gives Orange County homeowners and businesses peace of mind when hiring our team.

Do you provide interior and exterior house painting?

We specialize in both interior and exterior house painting services for homes and commercial buildings. Our interior painting covers walls, ceilings, trim, cabinets, and detailed finishes. Exterior painting includes stucco, siding, brick, and other surfaces designed to withstand California conditions.

How long does a typical painting project take?

Project timelines vary based on the size of the property, surface conditions, and scope of work. Interior painting projects may take a few days, while exterior house painting or commercial painting projects may require additional time. We provide clear timelines during the quote phase so you know what to expect.

What kind of paint products do you use?

We use high-quality paint products from trusted manufacturers, including Sherwin Williams and Dunn Edwards. Product selection depends on the surface type, location, and desired finish. Using the right materials ensures durability, color retention, and long-term performance.

Do you offer a workmanship warranty?

We offer workmanship warranties based on the paint products and services selected for each project. Warranty coverage is explained clearly before work begins so expectations are always aligned. Our commitment to quality workmanship stands behind every completed job.

Most painting projects begin within 1 to 3 weeks depending on the scope of work, surface conditions, and current scheduling availability.
